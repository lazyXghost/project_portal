About

What is Project Portal?

The Project Portal, as the name implies, is a centralised procedure for assigning projects to all interested undergraduate students. This allows them to work on projects that they are interested in inside their departments, as well as help initiate projects in their areas of interest. During the semester, it encourages students to participate in hands-on projects with IIT Mandi professors and their teams.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

The Project Portal, as the name implies, is a centralised procedure for assigning projects to all interested undergraduate students. This allows them to work on projects that they are interested in, as well as help initiate their own projects in their areas of interest. It encourages students to participate in hands-on projects with IIT Mandi professors and seniors.

When one applies through personal mailing, it is on him/her to track, contact and apply to various professors or seniors, whereas in a portal the student can select from the projects that have already been allocated to the portal and the student doesn't have to get involved in most of the internal work.

You will receive an email from the portal if your application gets selected for the project.

The project can be counted as “academic internship”, but will not count towards 2 credits of compulsory Industrial Internship.

It depends on the professor or senior who floated the project if he/she considers it an important factor while selection.
